Why HTML5

  • When Netscape 4 and IE 4 implemented CSS, their support did not match the W3C standard (or, indeed, each other).
  • Netscape 4 had horribly broken support.
  • The result is one browser may interpret a CSS property or HTML tag one way while another may have a slightly or even largely varied implementation.
  • And Web designers have long been plagued by the support (or lack thereof) of standards in the browser.

Lately

  • With the efforts of standards advocacy groups, browsers have been moving toward a more consistent support of the web standards.
  • And this is handled by introducing the Doctype switch!
